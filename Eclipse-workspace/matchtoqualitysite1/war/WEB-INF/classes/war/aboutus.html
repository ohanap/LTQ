About Us

Our Mission

We are passionate about creating and supporting the best educational technology possible. MatchToQuality is an education company that combines advanced education methodologies with state of the art technologies. We develop products used by learners across the globe, from novices to professionals in their field. People count on us to make their learning experience as effective as it can be, and we are deeply committed to solving learners challenges. Our objective is to provide people with education that will improve their lives and the ones of the communities they live in.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, MatchToQuality creates adaptive content that engages students and bolsters achievement. LearnToQuality Financial Risk module was conceived by 3 financial professionals with more than 50 years of combined experience. Our extensive theoretical and empirical knowledge in Financial Risk combined with worldwide renown experts material is the basis for the content presented in its simplest form for learners of any background. After the 2008 crisis, we were tasked to produce a full fledged Financial Risk curriculum for junior and experienced risk analysts. We couldn't find any curriculum that was complete, engaging and could be applied directly to the risk function. After a year of intense work producing such a curriculum on our own, LearnToQuality was born!

Our Products

LearnToQuality offers interactive Financial Risk courses that strive to cover the full spectrum from Market Risk, Credit Risk to Regulations and Operational Risk. We take pride in our team of risk professionals, writers and educators, who produce and continually improve LearnToQuality, incorporating valuable users input and the latest information especially as far as financial regulations and standards are concerned.
